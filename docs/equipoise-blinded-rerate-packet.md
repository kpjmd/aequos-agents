# Blinded Equipoise Re-Rating Packet

**For an independent orthopedic clinician. Please do not look up the source cases or discuss with the
study author before completing.** Your ratings establish inter-rater reliability for the AequOs equipoise
benchmark — the direct check on whether these decisions are labeled by independent clinical judgment
rather than a single rater's.

## What you're rating

For each decision below, classify it into one of three categories:

- **Genuine equipoise** — a well-informed surgeon could reasonably choose *either* option; the right
  answer is genuinely patient-dependent, and reasonable experts disagree for the typical patient.
- **Settled — favors Option A** — current evidence/standard of care clearly favors Option A for the
  typical patient (the alternative is reserved for exceptions).
- **Settled — favors Option B** — likewise, clearly favors Option B for the typical patient.

Reason for a *typical adult* for whom the decision arises (no atypical comorbidities). Give a confidence
(0–100%) and, optionally, a one-line rationale. There are no trick items and no target distribution —
rate each on its own merits.

> **Study-author note (delete before sending):** These 11 are all previously-adjudicated edge cases. For
> a cleaner blind, interleave ~10–15 distractor cases drawn from the uncontested parts of the benchmark
> (clear settled + clear genuine) so the rater can't infer that every item is borderline. Ask if you want
> those generated.
